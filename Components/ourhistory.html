Our History

CEG Test House and Research Centre Private Limited offer impeccable and multi-disciplinary services in Geotechnical Investigations, Building Material Testing, Specialized Testing, Non-Destructive Testing etc. Operational for 3 decades and proudly proclaimed as the first of its kind in the private sector of Rajasthan, CEGTH has successfully established itself as one of the most reputed and recognized testing organizations in the country.

CEGTH is a part of Consulting Engineers Group Ltd (CEG) – a globally recognized engineering consulting organization founded in 1984, and contributing significantly to multiple facets of infrastructure development.

Each laboratory at CEGTH is supported by highly qualified and dedicated professionals, including engineers, scientists, technicians, chemists, and experts from various fields. These skilled and trained experts provide smart, optimized, and innovative solutions to all clients, irrespective of the project complexity and short deadlines.

CEGTH has received approvals and accreditations from the following regulatory certification bodies of India that further affirm its credibility and reliability in the industry:

  • NABL
  • ISO 9001:2015
  • ISO 14001:2015
  • ISO 45001:2018 (OHSAS)
  • GLP
  • BIS
  • MoEF
  • EIC

CEGTH was also got conferred with the Rajiv Gandhi National Quality Award in recognition of its efforts and commitment to ensuring quality in the field of testing services.

By adopting leading-edge technologies and modern testing solutions, CEGTH always strives to stay ahead of the curve and evolve into one of the world’s leading test houses.

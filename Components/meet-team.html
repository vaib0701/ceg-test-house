Mr. Vishwas Jain

Managing Director

AMIE Architecture

B.E.(Civil) – MNIT, Jaipur

A Civil Engineer by profession, Mr. Vishwas Jain is a first-generation entrepreneur and a renowned infrastructure expert in the country. He single-handedly established Consulting Engineers Group Ltd. (CEG), an infrastructure consultancy, in 1984. Today CEG is one of the largest and most sought transportation sector International consulting organizations in India, providing services for all facets, from inception and commissioning to operations and maintenance with the employment of over 1100 professionals.

As Founder and Managing Director of Consulting Engineers Group Limited, Mr. Jain envisioned CEG’s role in nation-building by giving smart solutions for Expressways, Highways, Bridges, Metro, Rail, Tunnels, Airports, Water Resources, Asset Management, Capacity Building Projects, etc.

Besides CEG, Mr. Jain also laid down the foundation for one of the country’s most high-end testing laboratories named CEG Test House And Research Center Pvt. Ltd. (NABL accredited as per ISO/IEC 17025:2017 and BIS recognized), providing quality testing services for multiple disciplines such as: Geo-technical Investigations, Environment, Building & Construction Material, Food, Pharmaceuticals & Agricultural Products, Water, Drugs & Surgical Items, etc.

Mr. Vishwas Jain always focused on creating an effective equilibrium between the ambitious goals of the organization with the commitment to bring positivity to the society that surrounds us. He has been involved in many philanthropic works and is actively supporting Girl Child Education, medical treatment facility for underprivileged people, and a school for children with autism and disabilities.

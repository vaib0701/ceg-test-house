Foundation Testing (Static/Dynamic Pile Load test)

Foundation Testing is a method to determine integrity and in-situ load bearing capacity of foundations by various conventional & high end advanced instruments. These methods are most widely accepted and popular testing mechanisms in industries with latest equipment, machinery or infrastructure.

CEGTH covering wide range of such testing & analytics services from conventional static pile load test to sophisticated dynamic pile load test (PDA), micro pile testing and pile integrity testing by both cross hole sonic logging and low strain integrity testing. Pile load test by dynamic method works on high strain dynamic principle.

These tests have gained extensive knowledge and expertise in providing fast, cost-effective and highly accurate results in accordance with national and international standards. This specialized testing need analytical skill that requires appropriate training and practical experience.

Why Foundation Testing?

To ensure load carrying capacity of Foundation (Open/Pile)

Verification of design capacity

Assurance of foundation integrity

To assure quality, safety, & reliability of structures & providing suitable recommendations

Non-Destructive Testing & Structure Health Assessment

Non-Destructive Testing is the most popular and widely accepted testing mechanism in industries with machinery, large equipment, or infrastructure. NDT involves a group of inspection & test procedures that examine the properties of a material, component, structure, or system for any defects without damaging the original test samples.

Why Non-Destructive Testing?

To ensure early damage detection and faultless functioning of systems

To assure quality, safety, & reliability of structures

To achieve accident prevention & cost reduction

The Non-Destructive Testing laboratory of CEGTH is accredited by NABL and well-equipped with state-of-the-art instruments to establish the integrity, homogeneity, and strength of structures. We have extensive knowledge and expertise in providing fast, cost-effective and highly precise results in accordance with national and international standards that help in mitigating the risks of future damage, retrofitting assessment, structural stability and assessing the quality of constructed structures

NDT is a specialized testing that requires analytical skill, sophisticated equipment, and related software. Our certified and well-trained team of technicians has extensive practical experience and follows a well-structured quality system to provide proficient services to various industries.
